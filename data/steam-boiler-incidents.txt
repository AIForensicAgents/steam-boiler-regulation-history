3. 1840 -- Nov 07, steamboat Persian boiler flue collapse, MS River, near Napoleon, AR--  >25
12. 1854 -- Mar 13, steamer Reindeer, boiler flue collapse, Ohio River, Cannelton, IN   --40-46
Flues are inside of boilers, and when one or more collapse, hot scalding steam is released. Often this led to an
